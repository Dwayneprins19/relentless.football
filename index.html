Relentless Football.

Project Relentless is football driven project, driven to tell the incredible stories from the Cape Flats. Stories that connect the community, through the talent produced and the oppurtinities presented to the youth.

Discover Our Journey

Our Story

We are building more than a football team. We are documenting a journey. From acquiring a third-tier football franchise to attaining a Ground that we can call home in Mitchells Plain, this is about Resilience, Ambition, and Belief.

Real dreams. A community that refuses to be overlooked. A community who will fight for thier identity. Win or lose, this story deserves to be told.

Youth Development

At the heart of Project Relentless is our commitment to nurturing young talent. Through our youth development programs, we provide aspiring footballers with the training, mentorship, and opportunities they need to excel both on and off the field. Creating pathways for the next generation to achieve their dreams. From Local grassroots to the world Stage.

Youth teams kicking off soon.

Who We Serve

Community

Celebrating Cape Flats football culture and restoring pride through honest, transparent storytelling.

Players and Families

Creating player profiles, exciting match footage and memories that will forever be engraved in the community.

Be Part Of The Movement

Project Relentless is built by passionate people — players, creators, volunteers, sponsors, and believers in the power of football to change lives.
